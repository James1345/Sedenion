Sedenion

by James McMahon

About Sedenion

Sedenion is a project to bring an up-to-date package for dealing with Matricies and Complex numbers to the Java platform.

Sedenion aims to bring advanced algebra to Java natively, without the linking to old C and FORTRAN libraries that is often seen. Calculations are designed to be fast, safe and easy to use. Of course, understanding the mathematics is up to you.
